 Welcome to Link Up NJ!

Permanent Jewelry are chains that are custom fit to your body and then welded closed without the use of a clasp; making it "permanent"! We use a special welder to weld a jump ring shut, closing the chain while it's on you. There is a little spark but do not worry, you wont feel a thing. The whole process is completely painless.
