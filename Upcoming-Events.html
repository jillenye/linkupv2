Book Appointment for Pop Ups Events here: https://linktr.ee/linkupnj

1. Look at the list of pop ups, date & time to figure out which one is closest to you or are available to attend. List is on Instagram or website. More dates and locations will be added throughout the summer. Appointments are limited and will fill up fast.

2. Using the link in my bio, or the "BOOK NOW" button on my Instagram profile. Select which pieces you would like to get, you can select multiple (ex: gold bracelet, silver anklet). If you would like to get two bracelets, there are specific options for that. If you want a charm, select that option as well. Please choose accordingly. Each customer must book their appointment separately to ensure enough time for each person.

3. Then, choose the correct date for the particular location you are interested in attending. Please make sure you are booking for the correct date and time for the pop up you want to attend.

4. Check out, and you're all set! You will get a notification for your appointment. Any questions or if you need to cancel, let me know via DM or email. 

***If you could not get an appointment, please DM me and I will put you on a waiting list for the next pop up event for your location.
