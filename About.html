ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Julie Illenye and I am the owner of Link Up NJ; the first ever New Jersey permanent jewelry service.

Ever since I was a young girl I've had a love for jewelry. I would sneak into my mom's vanity, put on all her gold jewelry and pretend it was all mine. As an adult I invested in quality jewelry that I could leave on through hand washing, showers, and excersing that way I never had to take them off. That's were I had the idea to offer a permanent jewelry service. Jewelry is so special, and sharing this experience with so many people is cherished. I offer fully customized fitted gold chains  to make the experience as personal as possible. I can't wait to see what chains you choose!

I am a born and raised jersey girl, and after graduating college in 2019 I began working in healthcare. My favorite part about what I do is getting to interact with so many people everyday. Link Up NJ allows me to combine my two passions; people and jewelry!

Starting a business is an uphill battle, but I cannot be more excited to start this journey. Look out for upcoming pop-up shops near you, bring a friend, parent, sibling, or anyone special in your life to get matching permanent bracelets! 

xoxo, Julie
